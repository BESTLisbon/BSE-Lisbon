What is a BEST Symposium on Education?

    BEST Symposium on Education (BSE) is an event that aims to raise awareness on Education of technology students. In BSEs, students, local and international professors, experts from different industrial or research associations, higher education institutions and company representatives discuss educational matters and try to improve the European Higher Education system.

    The outcomes of the discussions are afterwards gathered in a final report with the objective of improving technology and engineering education across Europe. Through the analysis of different BSE reports, papers are submitted and, when accepted, presented in international conferences on Education such as SEFI AC, WEEF, IACEE World Conference, etc.

    The topics that are discussed can widely vary: they can be proposed by the international Educational Involvement Department (EduID) of BEST or be related to Educational Projects connected to the European Commission, where BEST is involved via EduID.

    This year, the topics covered by each BSE will be:

BSE Krakow:


- Internationalization of Higher Education
- Future Universities
- Open Courseware

BSE Lisbon:


- Skills needed in STEM professions
- STEM Pedagogy Education
- Diversity in STEM education

BSE Aalborg:


- STEM Education
- Project Based Learning
- Continuous Learning and Valuable Working/Career skills


As in any other BEST event, there are also social activities such as sightseeing and city rally, company visits and cultural visits.

Survival Guide

Don't forget to bring!

Here's some things you should bring with you:

  • Passport or National Identity Card;
  • Visa (If required);
  • Flight tickets;
  • Travel/Health Insurance (If you’re from an EU member country make sure you bring your European Medical Card);
  • Student ID Card / ISIC / EYC;
  • Medicines (Better safe than sorry);
  • Some pocket money for extra expenses (Euros);
  • A printout of this survival guide;
  • Comfortable and light clothes and shoes;
  • Sleeping bag;
  • Swimsuit, beach towel, sunglasses;
  • Sunscreen (trust us, you will need lots of it!);
  • Towels and hygiene kit;
  • Songs from your country;
  • Camera + PC cable (we wanna know all the gossips!);
  • Laptop;
  • Alarm clock (you better wake up on time!);
  • Some smart clothes for the official reception (you don’t need a suit or an evening dress, (unless you are the personification of awesomeness like Barney).

You can find all this and in our Survival Guide, as well as other useful information.
