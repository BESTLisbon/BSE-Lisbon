What is a BEST Symposium on Education?

    BEST Symposium on Education (BSE) is an event that aims to raise awareness on Education of technology students. In BSEs, students, local and international professors, experts from different industrial or research associations, higher education institutions and company representatives discuss educational matters and try to improve the European Higher Education system.

    The outcomes of the discussions are afterwards gathered in a final report with the objective of improving technology and engineering education across Europe. Through the analysis of different BSE reports, papers are submitted and, when accepted, presented in international conferences on Education such as SEFI AC, WEEF, IACEE World Conference, etc.

    The topics that are discussed can widely vary: they can be proposed by the international Educational Involvement Department (EduID) of BEST or be related to Educational Projects connected to the European Commission, where BEST is involved via EduID.

    This year, the topics covered by each BSE will be:

BSE Krakow:


- Internationalization of Higher Education
- Future Universities
- Open Courseware

BSE Lisbon:


Skills needed in STEM professions
STEM Pedagogy Education
Diversity in STEM education

BSE Aalborg:


- STEM Education
- Project Based Learning
- Continuous Learning and Valuable Working/Career skills


As in any other BEST event, there are also social activities such as sightseeing and city rally, company visits and cultural visits.

Lisbon

    Lisboa (Lisbon) is the capital and the largest city of Portugal. It is located in the western coast of the country, where Rio Tejo (Tagus River) meets the Atlantic Ocean. It is one of the oldest cities in the world, and the oldest in Western Europe, predating other modern European capitals such as London, Paris and Rome by centuries.

    Lisbon's trademark seven hills are peppered across the cityscape like lofty guardians of colour and history. Capped by a collection of terraces known as miradouros (viewpoints), a must-see web of no-filter-necessary views over Lisbon, the Tejo and beyond is formed. A city full of authenticity where old customs and ancient history intermix with cultural entertainment and hi-tech innovation, where you can feel safe wandering around day or night, with cuisine dedicated to creating over a thousand ways to cook the beloved bacalhau (salted cod).

    The third most hospitable city in the world (by Tripadvisor) and the coolest place everywhere (by CNN).

Lisbon's BEST Symposium on Education

    The BEST Symposium on Education in Lisbon is happening from the 1st to 12th of August, and there, you’ll have the opportunity to share your ideas and opinions on different topics in regard to higher education by participating in workshops, discussion groups and lectures from professors and experts in their respective fields. This year’s topics are:

  • - Skills needed in STEM professions
  • - STEM Pedagogy Education
  • - Diversity in STEM education



    Results and outcomes of the sessions will be presented in different conferences and published in papers dealing with higher education. If you'd like to contribute to the future of Europe and for your voice to be heard, this event is perfect for you. And all that in one of the most beautiful capitals of Europe, sunny Lisbon!
