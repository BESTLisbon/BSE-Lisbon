BEST

    BEST is an apolitical, non-profit NGO in constant growth. More than organizing events for students, BEST strives to strengthen the ties between universities, students and companies.

    Formed in 1989, BEST now counts with over 3300 members, spread out across 32 countries in 97 Local BEST Groups (LBGs).

BEST Lisbon

    Lisbon is one of the founding members of BEST, with its headquarters located in at Instituto Superior Tecnico, University of Lisbon.

   We are now counting with over 55 members, who have and will keep on delivering quality services to students from all around Europe. For more information about our LBG or BEST as an organization, go to (link_nosso_site) or to https://www.best.eu.org, respectively.
